What is Hypnosis?

Hypnosis, also known as hypnotherapy or hypnotic suggestion, is a trance-like state in which you increase focus and concentration. Hypnosis is usually done with the help of a therapist using verbal repetition and mental imagery.
When you are under hypnosis, you usually feel calm and relaxed, and are more open to suggestions. Hypnosis can be used to help you gain control over unwanted behaviors or to help you cope better with anxiety or pain. It is important to know that although you are more open to suggestions during hypnosis, you do not lose control over your behavior.

What can be treated with hypnosis?

Hypnotherapy is an effective method for dealing with stress and anxiety. In particular, hypnosis can reduce stress and anxiety, actively treat chemical dependency, addictions, bad habits, traumas, and phobias. It also helps to unlock limitations and insecurities, all without any risk.

Is it safe? Do I lose control over myself?

It's safe, the trance state is not the same as deep sleep. In trance, the person is extremely focused on something specific, but is not asleep. In the trance state, the patient is able to lead their thoughts and can remember everything they heard and said.

I can't go to the clinic, can the treatment be done remotely?

Yes! With the facilities that technology provides us, nowadays treatment can be done remotely and there are already patients who opt for this modality. It is worth mentioning that in treatment by call, one should be in a comfortable and calm environment.
